# Presentation: Nassau Candy Distributor - Logistics & Business Analytics

This folder contains the PowerPoint presentation (`Shipping_Analysis_Presentation.pptx`) for the Nassau Candy Distributor analytics project.

## Presentation Overview

The presentation is a comprehensive 12-slide deck documenting a data-driven analysis of logistics operations and business performance for Nassau Candy Distributor, a national distributor of specialty confections and fine foods in the USA.

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- Opening slide with company branding

### Slide 2: About Company
- Company overview: National distributor shipping from factories to customers across regions
- Key success factors: Customer satisfaction, cost control, business scalability
- Challenge: Limited route-level efficiency insights despite large shipment data

### Slide 3: Problem Statement
- Lack of visibility in logistics operations
- Issues: Unclear efficient routes, frequent delays, regional performance variations, reactive decision-making

### Slide 4: Objectives
- Analyze shipping lead time across routes
- Identify fastest and slowest routes
- Evaluate delay frequency
- Compare shipping modes
- Analyze overall business and logistics performance

### Slide 5: Data Cleaning
- Data type conversions
- Date formatting
- Lead time validation
- Removal of invalid/missing/duplicate data

### Slide 6: Feature Engineering
- Shipping lead time calculation
- Route creation (Division → State)
- Delay status and flags
- Ship mode grouping

### Slide 7: Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel: Data cleaning and pivot analysis
- SQL: Data querying
- Power BI: Dashboard creation
- Microsoft PowerPoint: Presentation
- GitHub: Documentation and version control

### Slide 8: Shipping Efficiency Analysis
- Route efficiency
- Fast/slow routes
- Ship mode comparison
- Geographic analysis
- Delay analysis

### Slide 9: Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard
- Sales vs profit analysis
- Product performance
- Division sales distribution
- Cost-profit relationship
- City sales performance

### Slide 10: Key Insights
- Some routes significantly slower
- High delay frequency in certain regions
- Express shipping faster than standard
- High sales not always high profit
- Logistics bottlenecks identified

### Slide 11: Recommendations/Conclusions
- Logistics performance varies by routes/regions
- Identified inefficient routes and delay areas
- Express shipping better performance
- Sales-profit correlation insights
- Data-driven improvements needed

### Slide 12: Thank You
- Closing slide

## Key Themes

The presentation demonstrates how data analytics can transform logistics operations:
- From reactive to data-driven decision-making
- Identifying bottlenecks and optimization opportunities
- Improving customer satisfaction through better delivery times
- Enhancing profitability through operational efficiency
